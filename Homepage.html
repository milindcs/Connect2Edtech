CONNECT2EDTECH

CONNECT2EDTECH, headquartered in Bangalore, is a dynamic and future-oriented company led by Karthik Gowda. The organization is committed to delivering excellence through a wide array of services that foster growth and innovation across multiple industries including HR, business management, education, and pharmaceuticals. With a purpose-driven mission and a strong focus on social responsibility, CONNECT2EDTECH specializes in professional training, HR solutions, recruitment, business consulting, and blue-collar workforce deployment. It aims to create a meaningful bridge between education and employment.

Vision

To be the leading platform for training and job placement, fostering career growth worldwide..

Values

Integrity, Innovation, Inclusivity, Excellence, and Social Responsibility.

Mission

To connect education with employment through skilled training and placement opportunities.
